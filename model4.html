BMW X5

The BMW X5 is a mid-size luxury SUV produced by BMW since 1999.

  • Engine: 3.0-liter BMW TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder
  • Horsepower: 335 hp
  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph in 5.3 seconds
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic
  • Drivetrain: All-wheel drive
