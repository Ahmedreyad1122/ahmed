BMW 3 Series

The BMW 3 Series is a compact executive car manufactured by the German automaker BMW since May 1975.

BMW 3 Series Details

  • Engine: 2.0-liter BMW TwinPower Turbo inline 4-cylinder
  • Horsepower: 255 hp
  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph in 5.6 seconds
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic
  • Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive
