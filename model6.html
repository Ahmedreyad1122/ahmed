BMW M3 Competition

The BMW M3 Competition is a high-performance sports sedan known for its powerful engine, precise handling, and aggressive styling.

  • Engine: 3.0-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder
  • Horsepower: 503 hp
  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph in 3.8 seconds
  • Transmission: 8-speed M Steptronic automatic
  • Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive
